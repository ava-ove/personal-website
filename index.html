About Me


Studying in computer science specialist program and physical science major program, I am fascinated by the problem-solving power of software technology and programming in interdisciplinary fields. through technology, computing power, mathematics, observations and physical principles I am amazed of what remarkable discoveries can be made about the unknowns of the cosmos!

Through a highschool astronomy club’s project, I built a basic python interface that finds the most efficient path of travel between two land points based on Google Earth elevation data and horizontal distances. The project taught me about the principles behind the calculations of path efficiency for rovers traversing Mars, part of what made discoveries on Mars available to us! This experience along with robotics and programming competitions kindled my interest in both fields I am currently studying.

My motivation comes from the excitment of building myself with the goal of being able to contribute to either or both fields through interdisciplicary research, software or technology development.
